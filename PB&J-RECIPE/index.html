PB&J Recipe!

Learn how to make a simple peanut butter and jelly sandwitch.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly

Instructions

  • Lay out two slices of bread.
  • Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  • Spread jelly on the other slice
  • Put the two slices together.
